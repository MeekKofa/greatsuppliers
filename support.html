Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Registration

  • How do I create an account?
    To create an account, click on the "Join Free" button at the top right of any page. Fill in your details and verify your email address to complete registration.
  • What are the benefits of registering?
    Registered users can save favorite products, contact suppliers directly, request quotes, and access exclusive member-only deals and services.
  • I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
    Click on the "Sign In" link and then select "Forgot Password." Enter your email address and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Buying & Sourcing

  • How do I find products or suppliers?
    Use the search bar at the top of any page to search by product, category, or supplier name. You can also browse by category on our Products page.
  • How can I verify supplier credibility?
    Look for the "Verified Supplier" badge on supplier profiles. You can also check their transaction history, customer reviews, and response rate.
  • What is Trade Assurance?
    Trade Assurance is a free service that protects your orders from payment to delivery. It ensures product quality and on-time shipment from verified suppliers.

Payments & Shipping

  • What payment methods are accepted?
    We accept various payment methods including bank transfer, credit card, and online payment platforms. Specific options may vary by supplier.
  • How does shipping work?
    Shipping terms are negotiated directly with suppliers. Common options include EXW, FOB, CIF, and DDP. You can also use our logistics services for door-to-door delivery.
  • How long does delivery typically take?
    Delivery times vary based on product availability, manufacturing lead time, shipping method, and destination. Most orders are delivered within 15-45 days.
